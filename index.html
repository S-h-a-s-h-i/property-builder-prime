Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have answers to help you navigate your property journey.

What is the typical timeline for building a custom home?

A typical custom home build takes between 8 to 14 months, depending on the complexity of the design and weather conditions. We provide a detailed project timeline before we begin.

Do you offer financing options for new builds?

While we are not a direct lender, we partner with several preferred mortgage companies and banks that specialize in construction-to-permanent financing.

Can I visit the site during the construction process?

Absolutely! We encourage scheduled site visits with your project manager to ensure safety while allowing you to see the progress firsthand.

What kind of warranty do you provide?

We provide a comprehensive 1-year builder's warranty on materials and workmanship, plus a 10-year structural warranty on the foundation and framing.
